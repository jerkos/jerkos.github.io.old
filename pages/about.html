About

Bioinfiormatics development

In the field of life sciences, companies offer researchers a multitude of ready-made software solutions. We do not follow the mainstream tendency that consists in proposing the miraculous tool that solves every data analysis challenge.

On the contrary, we develop open source programs tailored to meet your specific needs in a process of open innovation. Unlike proprietary solutions, the algorithms we provide are not a black box, their operational principle is known, proven by the scientific community and can be examined in detail. Furthermore you have all the rights on this software and you are trained on its use.

We focus on a tailored approach adapted to your specific needs and based on the knowledge and intelligence in data interpretation. The expertise of our services in bioinformatics and biostatistics is based on our knowledge in data mining, an experienced methodology, innovative solutions and a thorough understanding of biology and biotechnology.

Data mining

Optimize your R&D projects

We accompany you during collaborative services to achieve the processing and analysis of your clinical, biomedical or systems biology data. Working with us ensures that you define an operational study design, that you do not miss anything in your data, but also that you do not over-interpret them resulting in false positives.

You avoid hiring and training additional staff as well as undertaking a long-term commitment into a rapidly evolving area. Your costs are known through the establishment of a quotation and deadlines for completing your studies are controlled and reduced as our teams are directly efficient on your issues.

Reveal the true potential of your data

Our studies always begin with a framing phase to identify your needs and your scientific issues and thus offer the most appropriate solutions. Then we assist you throughout your project.

Expertise

Our team can also provide you with expert consultancy in bioinformatics and biostatistics. Our expertise covers a wide range of applications:

  • understanding of biological mechanisms.
  • proving nutritional or health claims.
  • discovery and validation of biomarkers in the context of pre-clinical and clinical studies: diagnostic biomarkers specific to a disease, susceptibility biomarkers associated with the risk of developing a disease, prognostic biomarkers for predicting the evolution of a disease and predictive biomarkers to predict the response to a given treatment. discovery of new drug targets. …

We specialize in the analysis and integration of large “omics” data sets – genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics and metabolomics – in the context of metabolic networks for personalized medicine and the development of companion diagnostic.
